Vegetables

What are vegetables?

Vegetables are parts of plants that are consumed by humans as food as part of a meal.

The history of vegatables:

Originally, vegetables were collected from the wild by hunter-gatherers and entered cultivation in several parts of the world

probably during the period 10,000 BC to 7,000 BC."
